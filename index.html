How To Play

Guess the VISDLE in six tries.

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter name of a KVISian.
  • Nickname, full names, and family names of teachers and students from batch 1-8 are allowed.

Example

P
A
L
M
A
  • P is in the word and in the correct spot.
  • M is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • A is not in the word in any spot.
